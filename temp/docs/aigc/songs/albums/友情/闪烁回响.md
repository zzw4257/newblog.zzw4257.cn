

_Sentimental Synth-Pop, Nostalgic AI-Core_

(Intensity drops slightly, but the driving beat continues. A clean, rhythmic electric guitar with a chorus effect enters, adding a new texture. Vocalist is more confident and reflective.)

当我搞砸了发布会 当我又被人误解

(Build-up is faster and more confident. Vocalist's voice is full of conviction and emotion. The arpeggiated synth becomes brighter and more prominent.)

[Chorus]

(Even more massive and euphoric. Add lush vocal harmonies and ad-libs. A subtle string section swells in the background, adding an epic, cinematic feel.)

[Vocalist: Whispered vocals, vulnerable tone]

[Synth Solo]

(An expressive, melodic synthesizer lead takes over. It's not fast or flashy, but soulful and lyrical, full of slides and pitch bends, like a digital heart singing a beautiful song. The full band provides a powerful, anthemic backdrop.)

[Warm synth lead, powerful drum and bass backing]
